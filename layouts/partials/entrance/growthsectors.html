Lincolnshire’s Investment Opportunities

Industrial & Technology Growth Sectors

Lincolnshire is leading the way in the high-growth, technology-driven sectors of the future.

Advanced Engineering & Manufacturing

A leading industry cluster with cutting-edge innovation in Industry 4.0 specialisations including AI, big data, robotics and automation.

Agrifood

At the heart of the UK’s agrifood sector and home to Europe’s largest agrifood technology, automation and robotics industry cluster.

Defence & Security

The location of choice for defence and security technology innovation, with 40% of the RAF’s assets in or near the region.

Logistics

A major logistics centre with the UK’s 2nd highest concentration of logistics businesses and the country’s 2nd largest ports complex (by tonnage).

Low Carbon Energy & Industry

At the forefront of the UK’s transition to Net Zero and a pioneering testbed for clean energy generation, storage and distribution.

Intelligent Robotics

Advanced, cross-sector AI/intelligent robotics capabilities, transforming industrial productivity through digitalisation and automation.
